Pancake Recipe

The following recipe will go step by step on what is needed and what should go in first. The steps will make sure of the ingredients and the order of how to make the pancake. Once you have all the ingredients, you can start making the pancake. After you have finished, you won't be able to stop eating them.

  • 1 1/2 flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/4 milk and 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1 Egg

Steps

  1. Start by making the pancake batter. In a large bowl, put flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Then whisk them together
  2. In another bowl, preferablly a medium size. Whisk together the milk, egg, and butter. Heat up the butter before adding it to the bowl.
  3. Add the wet ingredients(the medium bowl) to the dry ingredients(the larger bowl) and mix until just combined. Do not overmix, lumps are okay.
  4. Heat a nonstick skillet over medium to low heat and coat it with butter or oil of your chose. Use a 1/4 cup measuring cup to pour the batter onto the skillet. Cook until bubbles form on the surface and the edges look set, about 2-3 minutes. Flip the pancake and cook until golden brown on the other side.
  5. Once finished, you can add any extra to the pancake, fruits or maple syrup.
