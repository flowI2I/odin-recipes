Waffle Recipe

This recipe will guide you on how to make the perfect waffle of your dream. The steps will make sure of the ingredients and the order of how to make the waffle. Once you have all the ingredients, you can start making the waffle. After you have finished, you won't be able to stop eating them.

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea sealt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup butter melted 1 stick
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat a waffle iron. Lightly grease it with butter or spray oil of chose.
  2. In a large bowl, add the flour, baking powder, and salt. Then whisk them together.
  3. In a medium bowl, add the eggs, the milk, the metled butter, sugar and vanilla. Finally whisk them until well combined together.
  4. Add the ingredients from the medium size bowl to the large bowl. Mix until combined a few lumps are ok.
  5. Scoop 1/2 cup of batter for each wafle into the waffle iron. Cook for 4-5 minutes or until golden brown and lightly crisp.
